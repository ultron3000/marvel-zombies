MARVEL ZOMBIES

The animated horror series based on the popular Marvel Comics storyline

New episode every Wednesday

Season 1 (2024)

TV-MA 5 Episodes Animation, Action, Horror

In an alternate universe, a zombie virus has infected most of Earth's super-powered heroes and villains. A ragtag group of young survivors must navigate this apocalyptic wasteland, avoiding the infected versions of their former idols while searching for a cure. Based on the popular Marvel Comics storyline.
